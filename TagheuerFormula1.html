FUNCTIONAL AND ELEGANT



The notched and fluted racing bezel is in polished stainless
steel and aluminum. Super-LumiNova® applied indexes, large
“12” and hands for better legibility. The iconic screw-down
crown has “Easy Grip” finishing for better handling with driving
gloves.










SPEED AND RELIABILITY


The robust steel bracelet has a double
safety folding clasp to prevent accidental
opening and a “Driver” extension for
wearing over a racing suit. Water resistance
is up to 200m.


THE MEASURE OF SUCCESS


The three chronograph counters track
permanent seconds, hours, minutes and
1/10ths of a second. The precision
tachymeter scale on the bezel measures
converts a distance and into speed. The
watch itself delivers 100% function,
elegance fearless and sporting allure
throughout every moment of the day.
