Tudor

BLACK BAY


Prize: 18,770.00


TUDOR introduces the newest Black Bay; the latest technical and aesthetic
evolution of the emblematic diving watch with a burgundy bezel. A blend of
cutting-edge watchmaking technology and design elements inspired by TUDOR’s
nearly seven-decade heritage of making robust professional diving watches, this
model features evolved design elements, a “T-fit” clasp and is Master
Chronometer-certified by METAS.

In its third iteration, the new Black Bay is another example of
TUDOR's expert command of one of the industry's most
demanding standards in terms of chronometry and resistance to
magnetic fields. Its Master Chronometer certification, which
requires a substantial number of changes to a regular TUDOR
Manufacture Calibre, confirms the exceptional quality of its
watches.

This new model keeps the original proportions of a 41mm case
with a thinner profile and features a redesigned crown. The
unidirectional rotatable bezel offers very prehensible sides and the
seconds hand has been reworked to recalls the look of the early
diving watches of the brand with a lollipop design.
