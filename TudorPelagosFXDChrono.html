Tudor

Pelagos FXD Chrono


Prize: RM21,410.00


In celebration of its partnership with Alinghi Red Bull Racing, TUDOR unveiled two
new Pelagos FXD models inspired by yacht racing. With cases made from
carbon composite, titanium, and stainless steel and fitted with Manufacture
Calibres, these watches embody the daring spirit needed in the most competitive
yacht race in history. The watches are imbued with the spirit of TUDOR and
Alinghi Red Bull Racing; two separate entities that share the vision of pushing
traditional boundaries.

A long-term partnership between TUDOR and Alinghi Red Bull
Racing began in 2022, the first time TUDOR has ventured into the
world of competitive yacht racing. In this centuries-old sport,
victory comes by fusing a resolutely daring human spirit with
cutting-edge technology. The Pelagos FXD “Alinghi Red Bull
Racing Edition” watches were born out of this philosophy, by
combining a high-tech carbon composite, a first for TUDOR, with
titanium and stainless steel, just like on an AC75 (America’s Cup
75) hydrofoil racing yacht. These distinctive, high-tech sports
watches come with the Alinghi Red Bull Racing logo engraved on
the case back to further enhance the collaboration.

Using the Pelagos FXD case design, characterized by its fixed
strap bars and initially developed for the French Navy’s combat
divers, these new models both come with a bidirectional 120-
notch rotatable bezel that are graduated from 60 to 0, so as to
allow for easy tracking of countdowns, a key moment before the
start of a regatta. In the same hue as the hull of the AC75, the
matte dials put legibility first and foremost. These distinctive, high-
tech sports watches feature nods to this incredible partnership: a
subtle “Alinghi Red Bull Racing” mention on the 45° angled
rehaut, overarching the dial’s layout while the Alinghi Red Bull
Racing logo is engraved on the case back.
