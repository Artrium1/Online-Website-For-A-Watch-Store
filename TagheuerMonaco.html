MONACO

Automatic Chronograph, 39 mm, Steel


Prize: RM 31.800,00


This automatic chronograph Monaco Calibre 11 is THE
original model, the very first square and waterproof
chronograph in the world. Revolutionary and icon of the
"King of cool" Steve McQueen, this calibre 11 edition still
features the famous crown on the left; it was a way of
showing that manual winding was no longer necessary.
